A Recipe for Happiness


Description


This is a recipe for happiness!


Ingredients


  • (1) Grateful human
  • (1) Spirit of Service
  • (1) Handful of Good Friends
  • (1 or More) Purpose(s)
  • (1 or More) Love(s)
  • (1 or More) Passion(s)

Directions


  1. Perform (1) daily act of kindness (anonymously is best) without any thought of recognition or return
  2. Experience, Enjoy and Honor Nature
  3. Get Lost in Great Music
  4. Sing and/or Dance Like No One is Listening/Watching
  5. Learn to Respect Yourself and Others
  6. Do What You Love
  7. Care For and Preserve That Which Does Not Have a Voice (Animals, The Environment)
  8. Laugh Often
  9. Seek to Understand Rather Than to be Understood
